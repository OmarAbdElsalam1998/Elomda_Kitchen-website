About Us

Al-Omda Kitchens is an innovative institution established in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia in 2005, affiliated with the Sahel Al-Omda Trading Company. It brought together a group of more than 50 creative people with Arab minds and arms, taking it upon themselves to provide every Arab home with modern decorative touches from the finest wooden kitchens, as well as aluminum and all wooden decoration works, cabinets and sinks. Marble gives Arab homes the sense of luxury they deserve

18 years of creativity and uniqueness, and today we have two branches inside Riyadh, in addition to our factory, which is a castle for creative fingers.
